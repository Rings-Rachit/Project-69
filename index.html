Campus Life

school church

Circa 1978

The on-campus Xavier Church has a historic history. The church is known world-wide thanks to the visit by the pope to Goa. It's serves a choir room as well, with choir classes included in the school as both a class and an after-school optional extraciricular. This church has fostered many of the most prominent indian singers in the world, not to mention the amount of kids it set onto the way of god. This church has been a fantastic addition to the school in the years since its opening.

school library

Was built in 1985

This is the famous, library of the school. It house thousands of book dating back to the 1920's, free for all the students to use. Every kid is given a library card and allowed freedom on whatever they'd want to check-out and read. this revelutionary system has allowed for the school to perform well-above the national average, with the Prime Minister also allowing a visit to the school by his ministers. There are 5 floors' worth of books in the whole library, along with the newly-installed computers and digital database. The Xavier school library is ranked #1 for best library in India.

school auditorium

Finished in 1982

The school auditorium is the 3rd biggest in India and can house all of the school inside for functions, with a capacity of 5,000 people. A larger amphitheater is located nearby as well, allowing for 10x the capacity, at 50,000 people. The school auditioruim is the place where award ceremonies take place, as well as music auditions and school plays. The size of the schools meeting areas has made it the place where every year the city, district, and state competition ceremoonies are always held. It is also the place where the graduating classes get a final send-off.

Brought home silver medal in state sports championships

This competetion is an inter-school competition including sports like footbal, badminton, kabadi, etc. It is watched by millions globally and is the most prestigious competition a school can do well in inside the world of school sports.

This year, the respective teams for Math and Science both got podium in the international olympiad. The triple-talent prodigy, Rachit Srivastava, the star of the school, led both teams to victory with the school's brightest minds again, making the school the first to win twice in all history.
